This week, we're talking about time in music.
When it comes to writing down and reading information about musical time,
we have to learn to be both precise and also very expressive.
We're going to introduce you to a number of theoretical concepts to do with
duration and rhythm, and to do with feeling and form.
We'll also explain how we can use stave notation to communicate about these
musical concepts by combining graphic and symbolic information.
So, let's get stuck in!
