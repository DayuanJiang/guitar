[MUSIC]
This week, we will look at the way
triads and chords are constructed.
To help you understand their role in
our experience of key and tonality.
We'll also discuss how particular sequences of chords
feature in the creation of familiar musical structures.
Don't worry if you feel
the materials are complex at this stage--
that's totally normal.
Take your time and remember to
repeat and review things as much as you need.
As always, you'll grasp ideas more
easily by taking time out to play and
listen or sing along. And feel free to
share and discuss your ideas and questions in the forums,
it helps the whole learning community.
There are also lots of excellent music theory resources,
websites, YouTube channels, and apps, and so on.
So, remember that you can always cross-check
our explanations with theirs.
Sometimes we just need to hear an idea explained
a few different ways before we
understand it for ourselves.
